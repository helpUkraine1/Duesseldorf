Help Ukraine

Dear fellow citizens, horrific images from Ukraine overtake us every day. After the feeling of shock and powerlessness, we would like to make our solidarity contribution to humanitarian air in Ukraine. The Ukrainian Community in Düsseldorf is mobilizing and asking for donations in goods and money. Your donations in goods will be brought to the Polish border and will be distributed from there. In order to facilitate transport, we ask that all donations in kind be packed in boxes, sorted according to CATEGORIES, and please write the content clearly visible on it.

VERY IMPORTANT!!! We don't take CLOTHES anymore!

MEDICINE

  • SAT tourniquet / tying systems
  • CELOX haemostatic
  • Coniotomy kit for emergencies
  • First aid kits to take with you, no hard cases
  • Blood transfusion systems
  • Infusion systems
  • Infusion systems
  • Catheter
  • Bandage materials,bandage plasters in rolls and burn plasters
  • Disinfectant, sterile and non-sterile gloves

You don't have to pack medicine. We will still check it!

SHELF FOOD

(pasta, rice, canned food, instant coffee, tea, energy bars, dry fruits, canned soups etc). Please don't bring any GLASS PACKAGING, the only exception is baby food, but in this case a whole box/pallet securely packed.

HYGIENE ARTICLES

Shower gel, shampoo, dental and feminine hygiene,as well as diapers

INSULATION MATS, SLEEPING BAGS, THERMAL UNDERWEAR AND SOCKS

BATTERIES, FLASHLIGHTS, POWER BANKS, HELMETS, RADIO DEVICES, BAGS that can be filled with sand, TAPE, TENTS, BULLETPROOF VESTS CLASS 3 and higher, no matches please!

We still need your help with packing and loading trucks. If you know any logistics company that can provide us with transport, please call following number: +4915750080038 The packages can be handed in here: Church Heilig Geist, Moltkestr 63A, 40477 Düsseldorf We gladly accept your donation daily from 9 a.m. to 7 p.m We act transparently and in confidence. We thank you in advance for your generosity. Ukrainian community in Düsseldorf
